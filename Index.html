FootballLife — Privacy Policy

Applies to the FootballLife mobile game (Android / Google Play).
General audience • Ads enabled
Last updated: 2026-02-21

1. Information We Collect

The Game does not require user accounts and does not directly collect, store, or transmit personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Game progress and settings are stored locally on the user’s device using local storage technologies (including SQLite). These data never leave the device and are not accessible to us.

Tip: If you later add cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or backend user profiles — update this policy and your Google Play “Data safety” form.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Although the Game itself does not collect personal data, certain information may be collected automatically by third-party services used by the Game or by the platform, including:

  • device type and model
  • operating system and version
  • language and approximate country/region
  • app usage, performance data, crash reports
  • advertising identifiers (for ads and measurement)

3. Advertising

The Game displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These services may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to display ads and measure their effectiveness. Advertising partners process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game relies on third-party services such as:

  • Unity Technologies (game engine and related services)
  • Google Play Services (distribution platform)
  • Advertising services (e.g., Unity Ads, Google AdMob, or similar providers)

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. How Information Is Used

Automatically collected information may be used to:

  • display advertisements
  • monitor and improve performance and stability
  • analyze crashes and technical issues
  • improve overall user experience

We do not use this information to personally identify users.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Game is suitable for a general audience and does not contain content with age restrictions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that personal data of a child has been collected, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. Data may be processed by third-party services only as necessary to operate the Game (such as advertising and platform services).

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page. Continued use of the Game after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
